Resources

Research Component

Your vision of how the maker culture can make progress towards achieving your SDG should be supported by sound research. You may decide the format or style of the presentation on Demo Day. This document should provide background information on the specific aspects of the SDG you hope to solve. This will include who/ what may be affected by the issue, possible solutions seen in literature and how your strategy fits into these. You are encouraged to use graphics, graphs and visualizations to support your research.

Planning your project

The Resources below will help you to understand the circumstances around the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDG). This information can help you to choose your SDG, start your research and plan your project.

  • United Nations Sustainable Development Goals Report
  • United Nations Sustainable Development Knowledge Platform
  • United Nations Global Sustainable Development Report

Literature Sources

As with your academic studies we encourage you to review literature regarding solutions and issues related to your SDGs. Please look to UTSC's Research Guides by topic and University of Toronto Library's guide to articles and databases to find research databases related to your SDG

Data Sources

These sources of data or statistics can be used to plan your project, integrate into an application or create data visualizations.

The World Bank World Development Indicators Databank

  • Fantastic source of national and regional level data on development indicators including, but not limited to infrastructure, health, demographics, education and economics.

    • UNdata

  • The United Nations data portal where you can access data from a variety of agencies linked to the UN. A great source of data on a large variety of sources.

    • WHO Global Health Observatory data repository

  • A health related data source that is arranged in a way to monitor progress on the UN SDGs.

    • FAOSTAT

  • A datasource related to agriculture, its effects on the environment and food supply around the world.
