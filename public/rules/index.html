Rules

Team Formation

Students will sign up individually. On kick-off day, you will have the opportunity to pitch your ideas and listen to others' ideas to help you form teams. You are encouraged to form teams with students from various discipline of studies, up to a maxium of 7 students in one team.

Prior to #UTSCHackathon

To ensure fairness please do not begin work on code, design elements or business and marketing plans before the event. However, you are encouraged to research and brainstorm to come up with a pitch for recruiting team members on the kick-off day.

Data sources and other external resources

You are encouraged to use third party services, frameworks, APIs and open sources of data. If you and your team are not the original creator of the work, please maker attribution to the original author or licensor accordingly.

Code of Conduct

Demonstrate professionalism and be respectful of your fellow students and their ideas. Provide encouragement and constructive criticism for the benefit of the other attendees. University of Toronto is an inclusive environment and harassment in any form will not be tolerated.
