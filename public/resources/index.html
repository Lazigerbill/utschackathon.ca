Resources

Research Component

Your vision of how the maker culture can make progress towards achieving your SDG should be supported by sound research. For this reason we are asking you to submit a two page research handout/ report as part of your final presentation. This document should provide background information on the specific aspects of the SDG you hope to solve. This will include who/ what may be affected by the issue, possible solutions seen in literature and how your strategy fits into these. You are encouraged to use graphics, graphs and visualizations to support your research.

Planning your project

The Resources below will help you to understand the circumstances around the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDG). This information can help you to choose your SDG, start your research and plan your project.
