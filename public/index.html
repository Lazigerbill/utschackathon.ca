About #UTSCFinanceHacks

More people than ever can access the world's financial markets, thanks to recent advances in technology. While computers have made it faster and easier to participate in the markets, it hasn't helped to improve overall financial literacy.

Be the Solution

We are calling on all UTSC CMS and Management students to join. At #UTSCFinanceHacks you will create innovative software-and an accompanying business plan and marketing strategy to increase core competency in financial literacy. As a consequence, users will be empowered to improve their financial well-being and make better decisions on budgeting, investments, debt management, retirement planning and other major life events.

Check out the Rules

Who should attend?

Any currently registered CMS or MGMT students with any level of finance and programming knowledge. You don't have to be a pro to attend. If this will be your first hackathon, we can't wait to expose you to the world of creation! All it takes is a willingness to try something new. At #UTSCFinanceHacks, we bring in faculty members and industry experts to advise on your projects. Our goal is to bring people together to make new friends, learn new things, and bring new ideas to life.
